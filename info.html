FAQ

How long is the average wait time for the rides?
On weekends in the summer season, wait times can be upwards of 45 minutes for the most popular rides. It really depends on the weather on the day, sometimes it can be an hour but sometimes it can be 5 minutes or less.
Are there parking zones available? If so, how much do they cost?
We do have dedicated parking areas! These cost £4.00 for 2 hours, £6.00 for half a day and £12.00 for a full day.
Where can I get a ParkAlley map?
You can view the ParkAlley map on our site and download it from there. Or, you can pick one up after going through the entrance into the park. These will be given out for free at the information hut.
Are there cash points available?
Yes, we have 8 cash points dotted about the theme park. You shouldn’t have any trouble finding one!
If I leave the theme park, am I allowed to come back in on the same day?
As long as you keep your wristband on (you will receive it when you first enter), you are free to come back after leaving on the same day.
Do you allow pets in the theme park?
Unfortunately, due to allergies and phobias that some other visitors may have who visit the park, we do not permit pets to be brought onto the premises.
Can I bring my own food and drinks to ParkAlley?
Yes, you are allowed to bring your own picnic if you like. We also have dedicated picnic spots dotted around the park for places to sit and eat.
Do you have facilities available for people with disabilities?
Yes, we have disabled access to all of our rides and we also provide assistance if it needed. Just ask one of the staff!
Is there a place dedicated to dropping people off and picking people up?
Yes, next to the parking zones we have short stay drop off and pick up zones. These are free.
Where do I collect my tickets?
At the ticket office at the entrance you can give your name and reference number. We can then print off your ticket and give to you to go through the turnstiles. Or, you can print your ticket off yourself with the download link in the confirmation email and bring it with you on the day.
Do you offer a season pass?
We do offer a season pass! See our prices page for more info.
How old do you have to be to come for a day at ParkAlley?
Anybody is allowed at ParkAlley, but you must be 14 years old to come without an adult.
Can I go on rides if I am pregnant?
There are certain rides that you will not be able to go on while pregnant because of them having too much speed and winding turns. However, there are also rides that you can go on while pregnant. See our map for rides that are highlighted as being pregnant
